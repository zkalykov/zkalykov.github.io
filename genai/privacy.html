Privacy Policy

Welcome to GenAI! This Privacy Policy outlines our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide, including:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, and other information that you provide when you sign up for our newsletter or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience on our site. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our website
  • Notify you about changes to our website
  • Provide customer support
  • Gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our website
  • Monitor the usage of our website
  • Use data for training purposes, ensuring your data is anonymized

Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • With service providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers to monitor and analyze the use of our website.
  • For legal reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Usage for Training

We may use your data for training our AI models, but we will ensure that your data is anonymized. As this is a research project, your data will not be shared with any third parties.

Security of Your Information

The security of your personal information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
